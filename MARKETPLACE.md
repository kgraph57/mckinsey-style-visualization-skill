# Marketplace Listing Draft

## Listing Title

Strategy Consulting Visualization Skill

## Short Description

Turn business inputs into executive-ready strategy visualization specs with insight-led headlines, disciplined consulting-style layouts, data assumptions, and quality checks.

## Long Description

Strategy Consulting Visualization Skill helps agents create boardroom-ready visual specifications from raw business notes, metrics, and strategic questions. It guides the agent through decision framing, pattern selection, visual-system application, slide-spec generation, and final scoring.

Use it for board updates, competitive benchmarks, market maps, investment memos, strategic timelines, performance bridges, and executive summary visuals. The skill is intentionally lightweight: the entrypoint stays concise while reusable detail lives in progressive reference files.

## Target Users

- consultants and analysts preparing executive decks
- founders preparing board materials
- operators building KPI and performance narratives
- investors writing market or portfolio memos
- agent users who want reusable visualization workflows

## Category

Visualization, strategy, consulting, executive presentations, data storytelling.

## Keywords

strategy, consulting, executive presentations, board slides, visualization, data storytelling, competitive benchmarking, market map, waterfall chart, 2x2 matrix, investment memo.

## Key Benefits

- Converts raw notes into structured slide specs.
- Forces insight-led headlines and decision framing.
- Reduces generic chart output.
- Makes assumptions and data gaps explicit.
- Includes validation, examples, security notes, and roadmap.

## Proof Checklist
