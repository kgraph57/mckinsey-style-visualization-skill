# Board Update Input

## Scenario

A SaaS company needs a five-slide board update that explains growth, operational adoption, churn risk, and the recommended next decision.

## Raw Notes

- ARR increased from $10.0M in Q1 to $15.0M in Q4.
- New enterprise customers contributed $3.0M of incremental ARR.
- Existing customer expansion contributed $2.5M.
- Churn reduced ARR by $0.5M.
- AI workflow adoption increased from 18% in Q1 to 64% in Q4.
- The revenue forecast assumes implementation capacity can support 18 enterprise deployments next quarter.
- Current implementation capacity supports 12 enterprise deployments per quarter.
- Leadership is deciding whether to invest in implementation capacity before increasing enterprise sales targets.

## Constraints

- Audience: board of directors.
- Tone: analytical, concise, decision-oriented.
- Format: five slide specs, not rendered slides.
- Visual style: strategy consulting, 16:9 landscape, restrained palette.
- Data source: user-provided internal metrics.
